Agent Drift: Quantifying Behavioral Degradation in Multi-Agent LLM Systems Over Extended Interactions
-Abhishek rathor
Multi-agent Large Language Model (LLM) systems have emerged as powerful architectures for complex task decomposition and collaborative problem-solving. However, their long-term behavioral stability remains largely unexamined.
